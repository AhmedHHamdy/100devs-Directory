Gabriel Roela

Belo Horizonte, Minas Gerais - Brazil

Cohort 2

After 8 years of owning my own business I've decided to finally dedicate time to get professional about an old passion: Coding! After finishing Highschool my plan was to go into a CS college course down here in Brazil but life happened and I couldn't do it at the time. Then the pandemic hit and, during lockdowns, I took the new found freetime to learn some coding for fun which leaded me into participating in the Stanford University's program "Code in Place 2021" and it just made me even more intrigued by the idea of switching careers. Now I'm finally taking the jump to make this a reality through the 100Dev agency program.
