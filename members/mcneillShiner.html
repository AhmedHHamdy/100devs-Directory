I'm a community-taught software engineer with a deep love for small businesses, and the technology that enables them. In a past life I worked in furniture manufacturing & design, and the rush I felt while walking through the factories is very similar to the rush of constructing code: the raw potential in creating something useful and beautiful is the best feeling.


Fun Facts:

Needlepoint and interior design are my favorite obsessions, but I also dabble in sewing, knitting, and other forms of needlework. Also, always excited to talk about personal finance and all things urban design.
